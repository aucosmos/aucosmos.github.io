The intention

Technology as a doorway to tafakkur.

This is not a game, and the Names are not treated as features. The experience is a gentle prototype for slowing down, learning a meaning, reflecting, and returning to remembrance. It does not replace worship, study, duʿa, or the guidance of trusted scholarship.

The Universally Merciful

Mercy vast enough to surround creation: given before we notice it, present in what sustains, shelters, and allows life to unfold.

ٱلرَّحِيمُ

Ar‑Rahim

The Especially Merciful

Mercy felt as near, continuous care: not only surrounding life, but meeting a person within the details of it.

Recall one moment in which mercy felt personal and near.
