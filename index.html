About

AUCOSMOS is a space for exploring the intersection of cognition, faith, emerging technology, and human flourishing. Through research, reflection, and experimentation, I explore neuroergonomics, cognitive psychology, immersive technologies, human-AI interaction, and the practice of tafakkur in a rapidly changing world.
